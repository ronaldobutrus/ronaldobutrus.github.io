Domestic Electrical Installer

Jul 2020 - Aug 2020

Partnered with a building contractor, I joined an electrician in undertaking multiple electrical projects. The work included:

  • installing cabling to connect each part of the properties to the main board
  • designing electrical plans for each room
  • taking measurements and calculating positions in 3D

  • connecting devices such as switches, sockets, light fittings and fans.

British Airways: Speedbird-Z

Jan 2021 - Feb 2021

I've always had an interest in aviation, and I had always hoped to be able to complete some work experience related to the aviation sector. However, due to the SARS-CoV-2 pandemic that arose in 2019 and shaped our lives so differently, my work experience opportunity with British Airways was dropped.

British Airways introduced their online equivalent of the physical experience, launching their website: Speedbird-Z. I took the opportunity to complete their carefully constructed modules and watch a selection of webinars based on the following:

  • Insight into British Airways: an introduction to British Airways' history, career opportunities and information about their customer service
  • Application Advice: details about making a good CV, interviews and other job application advice
  • Confidence and Wellbeing: information about wellbeing, resilience and other stressful aspects of employment.
